About Us

What is CallnMail?

CallnMail is an email forwarding facility, acting through a website on the internet, as well as providing its services through mobile applications.

Why use CallnMail?

If you are a mobile phone user, you can register your mobile number with the CallnMail.com service, which in turn allows other CallnMail users and friends to email you, without knowing your personal or professional email id; all they need is your mobile number. In addition, when people are contacting you by phone, you can give out your email ID as the CallnMail.com email; using that specific mobile phone number, they contacted you from. CALLNMAIL is an email forwarding service that provides its services through mobile applications based on using the verified mobile number as an email id to forward email messages. Find any person by just knowing their mobile number. Users provide a valid email address , and a valid mobile number , CALLNMAIL verifies both and gives the option to the user to start using its features in order to forward all incoming email sent to the email country code (with “+”) followed by the mobile number under the domain CALLNMAIL (example +44mobilenumber@CallnMail.com ) is a mobile number from the UK.
