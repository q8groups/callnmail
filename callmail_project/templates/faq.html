CallnMail Frequently asked Question (FAQs)

What is CallnMail?

CallnMail is an email forwarding facility, acting through two channels: a website on the internet, as well as providing its services through mobile applications.

Why use CallnMail?

If you are using a mobile phone, you can register it with the CallnMail.com service in order for other users and friends to be able to email you, without knowing your personal or professional email ID. In addition, when people are contacting you by phone, you can give out your email ID as the CallnMail.com email. Using the CallnMail.com services, you can find out your friends email ID, without necessary knowing their personal email address.

Will I receive spam SMS or email?

We provide restricted access of our users’ data, for advertising purposes and to legal bodies, without discoursing the critical and sensitive user’s data like age, surname, photos etc. Advertising companies may contact you or SMS you under a certain criteria like, country, city, sex or age range, provided by CallnMail, according to terms of use. We are trying hard to prevent our users from receiving SPAM email and SMS.

What do you do for my security?

We take our customer’s security very seriously.

Is CallnMail FREE?

Yes basic use of CallnMail is FREE, CallnMail App is free to download.

How do I verify my email and mobile number?

During the registration step, simply click on get the activation code, and an SMS will be sent to your mobile number and you will be prompted to add on the website/app the activation code you received.

Do you provide a mailbox?

No currently, we do not provide a mailbox and we are not an email provider. But we are working on it.

I have changed my number or someone else registered my number

You can report to us any improper use of your personal mobile number by submitting a form on our website and our team will correct any improper use or number correction.

Changes to this Policy

If we change our Policy, we will post the changes on this page. Please check the website regularly for any changes to this Policy.
